How reviews get here

The honest pipeline.

  1. Marv binds a policy. Every client, every line.
  2. Two weeks in, a short email asks how it went. No prompts, no pre-written text.
  3. Positive replies get a Google Business review link. If a client agrees, their review — in their words — appears here and on Google.
  4. Negative or mixed replies get a phone call from Marv. That's how the agency improves. Nothing published unless the client asked.
