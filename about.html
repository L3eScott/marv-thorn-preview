Why independent

Captive vs. independent — the real difference.

A "captive" agent works for one company. If that carrier raises your rate, cancels your line, or refuses to cover flood — you're stuck with what they sell. An independent agent works with many carriers and switches you to the best fit whenever the market shifts. In Houston, where flood, wind, hail, and rate-cycle swings are constant, that difference is money.

Houston-local

Not a national brand pretending to be local.

Marv lives here. He drives the same freeways, weathers the same storms, and knows which carriers actually pay on flood claims when Buffalo Bayou spills. He serves Houston proper plus surrounding communities — Memorial, Bellaire, Sugar Land, Katy, The Woodlands, Pearland, Cypress, Spring, Kingwood — and he answers his own phone.

The promise

Four things Marv will not do.

  • He won't quote you the cheapest number that leaves you exposed. Real coverage first, price second.
  • He won't sell you a policy you don't understand. Every quote comes with a plain-English summary.
  • He won't sit on your renewal. If a better carrier fits at renewal, he tells you.
  • He won't hide behind a call queue. One person answers your phone. That person is Marv.
